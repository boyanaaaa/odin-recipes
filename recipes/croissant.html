Croissant with Nutella and Berries

Ingredients

  • 1 large croissant
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey/li>
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate hazelnut spread (such as NutellaÂ®)
  • 1/4 cup fresh berries, such as sliced strawberries, blueberries or raspberries

Directions

Step 1

Use a rolling pin or hands to flatten the croissant to 1/4-inch thick.

Step 2

Heat butter a medium skillet over medium heat until melted. Place croissant in skillet, bottom side down, and press firmly with bottom of a smaller skillet or saucepan until lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Drizzle honey evenly over croissant and add remaining butter to skillet. Flip croissant over and press again, cooking until the top side is lightly browned and toasted, about 2 minutes. Remove to a plate to cool.

Step 3

Spread Nutella over the croissant and top with berries as desired.
